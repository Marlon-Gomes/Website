      About the seminar

      Our goal is to bring together students in the Differential Geometry group Stony Brook to discuss topics in the interface between Geometric Analysis, Complex Geometry and affine areas. The seminar is jointly organized by  Marlon de Oliveira Gomes and Jordan Rainone.
